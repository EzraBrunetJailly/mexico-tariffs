Data sources

All layers on this map are based on tariffs as of March 1, 2025.

This map was created using data available from Mexico's National Institute of Statistics and Geography (INEGI), in addition to data from the following sources:

  • Mexican Business Registry, Directorio Estadístico Nacional de Unidades Económicas (DENUE)
  • Mexico's Quarterly Exports by State database (ETEF)
  • Mexico's National Geostatistical Framework (MGN)
  • Cargo Systems Messaging Service (United States Customs and Border Protection)
  • Harmonized Tariff Schedule of the United States (United States International Trade Commission )
  • The Merchandise Trade Balance of Mexico (BCMM)
  • U.S. Department of Commerce
  • U.S. Census Bureau
